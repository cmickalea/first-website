THE GROUP
Although friends for years, we were dubbed The Group in 2015. A like-mindedness brought us together and paired with shared interests, our bond was solidified. Individually we are intuitive, meticulous, ambitious, go-getters. Together- We are The Group. Four New York bred creatives with a love for fashion and culture. Visit our website for our thoughts, our outfits and our inspirations.
